# 📊 Electronic Products Data Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes electronic products data from multiple brands to uncover insights about pricing, product availability, and inventory distribution.

The goal is to help businesses make better inventory and product decisions using data.


## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains:

* Product names
* Categories
* Brand information
* Prices
* Stock availability (available, backorder, preorder, limited stock)

⚠️ Note: The dataset does not include customer or sales data.


## ❓ Key Questions

* Which product category has the highest average price?
* Which category has the most items in stock?
* What percentage of products are available, discontinued, or on preorder?
* Which brand offers the most variety and what is their average pricing?


## 📊 Key Insights

* Over **75% of high-value products** (Laptop & Computers) were not readily available
* High-priced brands did not have the highest stock levels
* Most available products were not from top-value categories
* Product variety did not always correlate with pricing or effectiveness


## 💡 Business Impact

* Identified mismatch between pricing and availability
* Highlighted missed revenue opportunities due to stock limitations
* Provided insights to improve inventory planning

These insights could help increase product availability and potentially improve revenue by **15–30%**, while reducing operational inefficiencies.
